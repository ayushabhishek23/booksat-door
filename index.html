One District-One Product”, Jammu & Kashmir

The J&K Government’s One District-One Product Programme aims to encourage such indigenous and specialized products and crafts in J&K that are found nowhere else – like the ancient and nutritious 'kashmiri roti ' ,
the rare and intriguing craft kashmiri Handmade shawls ( pashmina shawls ), .
This work has been a part of the valley since the time of Mughals .and wooden works, silverware, and papier mache are some of jammu exquisite arts and crafts.

Map Of Jammu And Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir's ODOP program is a vibrant showcase of the region's unique crafts and products. It's a burst of local pride, empowering artisans and entrepreneurs while preserving traditional skills. This initiative not only boosts the local economy but also celebrates the rich cultural heritage of the area, district by district.
